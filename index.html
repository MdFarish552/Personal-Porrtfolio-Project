About Me

The fact that frontend developers are full-time developers who produce an output that is visually appealing (thanks to the designs provided by others) sometimes confuses others, making them believe that frontend developers aren't really coding. However, that couldn't be further from the truth. As a frontend developer, you'll be coding all the time. While in some companies, the frontend developer is also a skilled designer or UX engineer,those are not the typical profiles. As a frontend dev, your learning focus should be coding-related (i.e coding best practices, software design patterns, frontend architecture, etc).
